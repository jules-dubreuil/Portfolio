InTempo: Master Your Speech
Real-time AI coaching to enhance public speaking, non-verbal communication, and message delivery.
Public speaking is a major source of anxiety for many. Whether it’s a professional pitch or a student presentation, the ability to convey a message clearly while managing stress is key. InTempo was designed to be a personal vocal coach that follows you everywhere. It’s an innovative app that analyzes your speech in real-time to provide immediate feedback and detailed post-performance analytics.
How can technology help speakers identify their vocal tics, manage their stress, and improve their non-verbal communication without the need for an expensive physical coach?
An AI-powered companion that monitors vocal and non-verbal performance in real-time, providing speakers with immediate guidance and advanced analytics to perfect their delivery.
