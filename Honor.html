HONOR 2026: The Digital Artefact
Humanizing the mobile interface through emotional and personalized AI.
In a world saturated with constant notifications, the lock screen is often perceived as a purely functional or even anxiety-inducing space. In collaboration with Honor, this 2026 prospective project aims to transform this static space into an interactive and meaningful experience. The goal is to forge a strong emotional bond between the user and their device, shifting the smartphone from a passive tool to a "living" digital companion.
How can AI and emerging technologies transform the smartphone screen into a useful, ultra-personalized, and emotional experience specifically designed for Gen-Z?
The concept is centered around an interactive virtual companion. This "artefact" evolves according to the user's preferences through deep customization options (ranging from 3D Kawaii aesthetics to Pixel Art). Beyond visuals, the solution incorporates a social dimension: users can share their creations or explore a community library, turning the smartphone into a true fashion statement and a unique tool for self-expression.
